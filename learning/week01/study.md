# Week 01 Study Guide

## Goal
Understand baseline chart design principles and how AI can support early ideation.

## AI-Guided Study Steps
1. Review `context/dataviz_principles.md`.
2. Ask AI to propose 3 chart options for one simple dataset.
3. Compare options using `context/chart_design_checklist.md`.
4. Write one paragraph explaining your final choice.

## Reflection Prompt
What did AI suggest that you kept, and what did you reject based on visualization principles?
