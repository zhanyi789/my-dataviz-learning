# Course Overview

## Course Title
Generative AI Applications in Data Visualization

## Course Purpose
This course teaches students how to use generative AI to design, critique, and improve data visualizations responsibly.

## What Students Will Learn
- Core principles of effective data visualization.
- How to prompt AI for chart ideas, revisions, and critique.
- How to document datasets and design choices clearly.
- How to combine human judgment with AI assistance.

## How AI Will Be Used in the Course
AI will be used as a design assistant, critique partner, and study coach. Students will use reusable context, prompts, and agent roles to produce consistent, explainable visualization work.
