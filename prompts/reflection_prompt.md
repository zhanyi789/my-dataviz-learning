# Reflection Prompt Template

## Context
I completed a chart draft and received critique.

## Task
Help me write a concise reflection on design decisions and revisions.

## Input
- Original chart idea: [insert]
- Feedback summary: [insert]
- Revisions made: [insert]

## Output Format
- What I changed
- Why I changed it
- What I would improve next

## Constraints
Maximum 180 words. Focus on reasoning, not just description.
