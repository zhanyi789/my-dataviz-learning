# Data Storytelling Framework

## Step 1: Define Audience and Decision
Who is the audience, and what decision should the chart support?

## Step 2: Identify Core Insight
What is the most important pattern, comparison, or trend?

## Step 3: Choose Narrative Structure
Use setup -> evidence -> takeaway.

## Step 4: Design Supporting Visual
Select chart type, annotations, and emphasis based on the insight.

## Step 5: Review and Revise
Test clarity with critique criteria and iterate.
