# Chart Critique Framework

## 1. Purpose Fit
Does the chart answer the stated question?

## 2. Visual Clarity
Are titles, labels, legends, and marks easy to read and interpret?

## 3. Data Integrity
Are scales, encodings, and transformations accurate and fair?

## 4. Narrative Strength
Is the key message clear, focused, and audience-appropriate?

## 5. Actionable Revision
List 3 concrete improvements in priority order.
